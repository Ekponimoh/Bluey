Privacy Policy

At The Designer George, your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website.

1. Information I Collect

We do not collect personal information unless you voluntarily provide it via our contact form or email. This may include your name, email address, and the message you send.

2. How I Use Your Information

I use the information you provide only to respond to inquiries, start projects, and improve my services. I do not sell or share your personal data.

3. Google Fonts

Our site uses Google Fonts to display consistent, visually appealing text. When you load a page, your browser requests the necessary font files from Google’s servers. This request may include your IP address, which is handled under Google’s own Privacy Policy. Google Fonts does not set cookies for serving fonts.

4. Third-Party Services

If I use analytics or embedded media, those services may collect their own data according to their policies.

5. Data Security

I take reasonable steps to protect your information, but no internet transmission is 100% secure.

6. Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted here.

If you have questions, please contact me.
